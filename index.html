Who We Are


YOUR chAnnel, is a page created to apply the theoretical study in a practical way. Here I randomly chose a theme, I ended up choosing baseball.


Currently, July 14, 2022, the page has only CSS and HTML in its source code, unfortunately it is not yet a responsive project. However, executing this project was challenging and interesting at the same time, look at the ticked boxes and the radios, they are customized, we have some placements made using the property, display flex.


For the future, the goals are: 1- Make the page responsive; 2- Apply a JavaScript, so that the purchase of the ticket and food is actually processed and returned on another page, or even at the end of this page, as if it were a proof of purchase; 3- Of course, to do this without estarting the page from scratch, let's just adjust the necessary points.


That's it for now, I'm happy with the result obtained so far, considering that I've only been studying for a month. See you on a new project, bye.
